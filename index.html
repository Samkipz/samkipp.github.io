About The Alma

The Alma is a signature lifestyle development in the fast growing satellite town of Ruaka that encompasses 1,2 and 3 bedroom apartments with impeccable finishing. The Alma envisions convenience and comfort. Available amenities include a retail centre, club house, gym, swimming pool and kindergaten. The Alma, which is set for completion in 2018 is 50% sold. Early stage investors in The Alma, have recorded annualized capital gains as high as 55%, since purchasing their units less than a year ago.
